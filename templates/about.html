Learning Philosophy and Team

At Learn English Online, we teach International English rather than British, American, Indian or any other type of English. Why?

  • You will often be in a situation where none or only some of a study group or group of colleagues are native English speakers.
  • We believe that focusing on clear, accurate and transparent English is the best route to fluency and making yourself understood.
  • Native English speakers might share the same language, but there is an enormous range of accents around the globe: there is no 'one' right way to speak English.
  • So worrying about what 'type' of English you should study is purely a distraction.
